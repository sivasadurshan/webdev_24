Grocery Store Loyalty Registration

Platinum

The Platinum Scheme is our top-tier loyalty program, offering premium benefits like priority customer service, early access to promotions, and significant discounts. Platinum members earn double loyalty points on all purchases, perfect for frequent shoppers seeking maximum savings and exclusive rewards.

Gold

The Gold Scheme provides a great balance of rewards and savings. Gold members enjoy substantial discounts, earn 1.5 times the loyalty points, and get early access to special promotions. It's ideal for regular shoppers who want added value and exclusive deals.

Silver

The Silver Scheme is our entry-level loyalty program, offering moderate discounts and loyalty points on every purchase. Silver members also receive advance notifications about sales and promotions, making it perfect for occasional shoppers looking for additional benefits.
